Training

Domestic Training Session

  • DATA conducts trainings on data collection methods, survey techniques, survey tools, data entry, data and data cleaning.
  • For all of the study we undertake, DATA facilitated the trainings to the coordinators, supervisors and enumerators. Beside these on job training,we facilitated several trainings for World Vision Bangladesh,CARE-Bangladesh and BRAC.
  • In World Vision DATA trained 24 staffs of the World Vision Bangladesh on how to conduct a survey and on survey tools in Savar BCDM.
  • In CARE-Bangladesh, DATA gave training on Anthropometric data collection to around 40 enumerators in Jessore.
  • In BRAC, DATA trained 16 NGO members on data entry, cleaning and reporting.
  • Our training expertise especially on conducting socio-economic surveys, data collection, data entry and data management.
